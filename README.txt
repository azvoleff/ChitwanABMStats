Chitwan_R_files - README.txt

This folder contains the files for calculating a number of statistical models 
and descriptive statistics necessary for parameterizing the Chitwan Valley 
agent-based model, and for work on climate change and migration in the Chitwan 
Valley.

The code was constructed by Alex Zvoleff and Li An at San Diego State 
University (SDSU) in the Department of Geography. Contact Alex Zvoleff or Prof. 
Li An at SDSU with any questions.
